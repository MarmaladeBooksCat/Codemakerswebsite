ABOUT THE RESTAURANT

Tea, Sandwiches and more is a eat-in, takeaway, and order-in restaurant. Specializing in healthy, delicious, and organic food that can be eaten quickly, Tea, Sandwiches, and More is your go-to place for good food.

TEAS

As it says in the name, Tea, Sandwiches, and More has a variety of teas for whenever you want a cuppa. Why not have an English Afternoon with your lunch, or maybe a Decaffeinated Earl Grey after dinner? We have herbal, as well as black teas. Our teas are soothing and you will probably want to have a cup of tea!


COFFEES

Our coffees are organic and will keep you ready for your day!


JUICES

Our juices are all made on the premises with fresh fruits, vegetables, and dairy (in the smoothies). Refreshing and healthy, they are a great way to keep cool in warm weather or have at breakfast.


SANDWICHES

Tea, Sanwiches, and More's filling sandwiches are good for any meal or on the go. With fresh bread, tomatoes, and other ingredients, our sandwiches are a delicious option.
